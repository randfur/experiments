# 2026-03-30
- Training page for quick recall of handbell position updates based on place notation.
- Place notation like X, 14, 12.
- User has a L/R switch to say which hand is in front.
- User presses the place numbers they should be at after a place notation.
- Different timing modes:
  - No time pressure.
  - Graded time.
  - Fixed timer, progresses without you.
