# 2026-06-27
- Visual:
  - World:
    - Ring of segments, like a bracelet.
  - Multiple camera views:
    - Following a bot.
    - Free form flying over world.
    - Overall world view.
      - Level of information detail changes with scale based on screen size of segments.
      - Can zoom into top section and spin world around.
  - Segment:
    - A square of land.
    - Shop building randomly placed on land.
    - Type of segment is based on what it buys/sells.
    - Type determines overall colour.
    - Random trees/shrubs/rocks with different densities and sizes, consistent across same type.
  - Bot:
    - Simple box car model.
    - Different accent colours across bots.
    - Movement avoids shop buildings.
      - Smooth bezier paths.
      - Sideways acceleration determines angle of car, always facing towards direction of acceleration.
      - Not real physics, just visualisation.
    - Buy/sell telegraphed with floating transaction details label.
