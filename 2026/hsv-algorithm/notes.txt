- HSV -> RGB algorithm.
- Simple approach:
  - The H angle gets a point on a circle around the axis 1,1,1.
    - Perpendicular vectors are easy to find:
      - 1,1,-1
      - 1,-1,1
  - Scale the values such that the max value is 255 and the min value is 0.
- Decreasing V decreases the max value.
- Decreasing S increases the min value proportionally within the available space.
  - Not sure about this one, needs experimentation.
- Implemented algorithm, it works.
- AI reviewed it and pointed out a flaw, the RGB will change with trig curves while the usual conversion algorithm is piecewise linear, this may have similar peak values but the inbetweens will be skewed.
